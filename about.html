About our company

Know the people behind the trust

Who we are

R.K. Finance Group Serves was established in 2010 by Kuldeep Sharma with a clear vision: to make finance accessible, transparent, and hassle-free for every individual in Madhya Pradesh. What started as a small venture in Indore has now grown into a trusted name for vehicle loans, used vehicle financing, and insurance services across the state.

We combine local insight with professional expertise. Our team understands that every customer has unique needs — whether it's a first-time bike buyer or a family looking for a reliable used car. That's why we focus on personalised solutions delivered at your doorstep.

Founder story

Kuldeep Sharma, a finance enthusiast with over a decade of experience in banking, noticed the struggle of common people with excessive paperwork and hidden charges. In 2010, he left his corporate job to start R.K. Finance with a single principle: "Loan chahiye? Phone laga'iye" — a promise of simplicity and direct communication. Today, that principle remains our foundation.
