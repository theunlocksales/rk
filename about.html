Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Vehicle Finance Across Madhya Pradesh

R.K. Finance Group Services was established in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision — to make vehicle financing accessible, affordable, and hassle-free for every individual and business in Madhya Pradesh. What started as a small operation in Indore has grown into one of the region's most trusted names in vehicle loan facilitation.

Over the past 15+ years, we have served more than 10,000 customers, disbursed over ₹500 crore in vehicle loans, and built partnerships with 50+ leading banks and NBFCs. Our commitment to transparency, speed, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our growth.

"Loan Chahiye Phone Lagaiye" — Our tagline reflects our philosophy of making finance as easy as a phone call. We bring the entire loan process to your doorstep.

We specialize in new vehicle loans, used vehicle financing, vehicle insurance, loan against vehicle, and commercial vehicle finance — covering everything from two-wheelers to heavy commercial trucks.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Kuldeep Sharma

Founder & Managing Director

With over 15 years of deep expertise in vehicle finance, Kuldeep Sharma founded R.K. Finance Group Services with a mission to simplify the loan process for common people. His hands-on approach and understanding of the local market has been the driving force behind the company's remarkable growth.

Starting from a single desk in Indore, Kuldeep Sharma has built a network that spans the entire state of Madhya Pradesh. His philosophy is rooted in building trust through personal relationships, transparent dealings, and ensuring every customer gets the best possible loan terms.

Under his leadership, R.K. Finance Group Services has become a preferred financing partner for thousands of families and businesses, working with over 50 banking and NBFC partners to offer unmatched choice and competitive rates.

My goal has always been simple — when someone needs a vehicle loan, they should think of us first. Not because of advertising, but because of the trust we've earned through honest service.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

A look at the key moments that have shaped our journey from a small office in Indore to a pan-M.P. finance services company.

10

2010 — The Beginning

R.K. Finance Group Services was founded by Kuldeep Sharma in Indore with a focus on vehicle loan facilitation. Started with partnerships with 3 banks.

13

2013 — Used Vehicle Division

Launched dedicated used vehicle financing services to address the growing demand for pre-owned vehicle loans in Madhya Pradesh.

16

2016 — Insurance Services Added

Expanded into comprehensive vehicle insurance services, offering customers a one-stop solution for all their vehicle financing and protection needs.

19

2019 — 5000+ Customers Milestone

Crossed the mark of 5,000 satisfied customers and expanded partnerships to 30+ banking and NBFC partners across Madhya Pradesh.

22

2022 — New Office at Orbit Mall

Moved to a larger, modern office at Vijay Nagar, Orbit Mall, Indore — reflecting the company's growth and commitment to professional service.

25

2025 — 10,000+ Customers & Counting

Surpassed 10,000 customers with ₹500 Cr+ in total disbursements. Now one of the most trusted finance facilitators in all of Madhya Pradesh.
