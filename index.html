FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common queries our customers have.

What documents are required for a vehicle loan?

You typically need Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, income proof (salary slips or ITR), address proof, and vehicle-related documents. For used vehicles, RC book and insurance copy are also required. Our team guides you through the exact requirements.

How long does the loan approval process take?

Most loans are approved within 24–48 hours of complete document submission. In many cases, we can provide same-day approval for straightforward applications with complete documentation.

What is the interest rate for vehicle loans?

Interest rates vary depending on the vehicle type, loan amount, and your credit profile. We work with multiple banks and NBFCs to get you the most competitive rate available — typically ranging from 8.5% to 14% per annum.

Do you provide loans for used/second-hand vehicles?

Yes! Used vehicle financing is one of our specialties. We provide loans for pre-owned cars, bikes, and commercial vehicles with attractive rates and proper vehicle valuation support.

Do you serve areas outside Indore?

Absolutely! While our head office is in Indore, we serve customers across all of Madhya Pradesh including Bhopal, Ujjain, Dewas, Ratlam, Dhar, and many more cities and towns.
